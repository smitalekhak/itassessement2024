Welcome to Ozeio Gallery

Located in the heart of Thapathali, Ozeio Gallery showcases art from both local and world renowned artists. Our gallery offers a unique blend of creativity and cultural expression.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a vibrant art community by connecting artists with art lovers. We strive to:

  • Promote Emerging Artists: By providing a platform for new and upcoming artists, we support the growth and development of the contemporary art scene.
  • Celebrate Diversity: We embrace a wide range of artistic expressions and cultural backgrounds, reflecting the rich tapestry of the global art community.
  • Engage the Public: Through exhibitions, workshops, and events, we aim to make contemporary art accessible to everyone, encouraging a deeper understanding and appreciation of the arts.
